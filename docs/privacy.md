# MyTasks Privacy Policy

_Last updated: November 28, 2025_

MyTasks is a fully offline task manager. We designed the app so that your data never leaves your device. This policy describes how the app handles information.

## Data Collection
- MyTasks does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
- There are no analytics SDKs, crash reporters, advertising networks, or telemetry services embedded in the app.

## Local Storage
- All tasks, preferences, and theme selections are stored locally on your device using the platform's secure storage APIs.
- Removing the app from your device deletes all stored data. Reinstalling the app starts with a clean slate.

## Network Access
- MyTasks works entirely offline. It does not access the internet, sync data to servers, or communicate with third parties.

## Third-Party Services
- The app does not integrate with any external services or APIs. There is no account system and no cloud storage.
